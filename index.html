About the system

verify.e-gov.am (renamed from e.verify.am on 01.09.2022) — a unified system for checking the validity of official documents issued by state bodies of the Republic of Armenia.

The system aims to make the online authentication process more efficient and improve the quality of services provided by state bodies.

The site currently has the ability to verify the validity of the following documents:

  • State registration certificates of civil status acts
  • Apostille-certified documents
  • Birth and death medical certificates issued by medical institutions of the Republic of Armenia
  • Reference to family status
  • Criminal record certificates
